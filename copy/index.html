Robotics & Automation Society

This IEEE society works to improve the theory and practise of robotics and automation engineering, as well as the associated arts and sciences, and to promote high professional standards among its members, all while paying special attention to the Society's Field of Interest.

Computer Society

The IEEE Computer Society is dedicated to the advancement of computer and information processing science and technology in theory, practise, and application. Its goal is to create a forum for those who want to advance their careers or obtain knowledge in the field of computer science.

Women in Engineering

IEEE WIE is among the world's leaders in engineering reformation. Its connects around 20,000 members from all over the world to help women advance in technology at all stages of their lives. IEEE WIE members form lifelong friendships, gain influential mentors, and make a positive difference in the world.
